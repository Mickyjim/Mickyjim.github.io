CryptoCoinz

CryptoCoinz enables you to keep track of up to 250 coins. This app offers the user the possibility to discover the world of crypto currency with valid and complete information. The app includes detailed information about each currency with overviews, charts and additional information with links to each individual website. Investigate on the evolution of each cryptocurrency with detailed charts to help you in your inquiries. Create a custom selection for your portfolio of coins you wish to keep track of.

Tutifruti

Tutifruiti is an app that let's you discover the virtues of certain fruits and dried fruits that you find in the fruit market and/or in certain places in the world. Especially that some are uncommon and intriguing. Each fruit is accompanied by a full description of it's origins, health benefits and a detailed nutritional value per 100g of vitamins, minerals, calories, sugar, fat and proteins. This app gives you knowledge of what you eat in times of great questions about what we consume on a day to day basis.
